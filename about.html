CREATIVITY IS WITHOUT BOUNDS.

Having a passion to explore the freedom of visual design and putting my hands to all things creative is the rhythm in my step. Growing up as a competitive basketball player I trained myself to be a strong multitasker. Being a designer I find myself still using this skill. I envision designs that communicate strong stories, create videos that catch a viewers attention, and post coffee updates on Instagram for 4 businesses all while eating a single peanut butter cookie (okay, maybe three cookies). The past few years have been a very colourful time of my life full of experiments with all mediums of creativity. It was during this time of my life that I found my Prince Charming and swapped my basketball shoes for a Macbook pro.
